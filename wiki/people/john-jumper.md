## Research areas

Protein and biomolecular structure prediction; deep learning for computational biology; AlphaFold series.

## Key papers

- [[accurate-structure-prediction-biomolecular-interactions-alphafold]] — corresponding author, AlphaFold 3 (Nature, 2024).

## Recent work

- AlphaFold 3 (2024): co-led the architecture redesign that replaced the AF2 evoformer with the pairformer and the AF2 structure module with a diffusion module, extending the system to nucleic acids, ligands, ions, and modified residues.

## Collaborators

- Demis Hassabis, Max Jaderberg, Pushmeet Kohli, Victor Bapst, and the Google DeepMind / Isomorphic Labs structure-prediction group.

## My notes

Jumper led the AF2 effort and was again a corresponding author on AF3. The continuity of architectural taste — from invariant point attention to diffusion-based denoising — suggests an unusually high tolerance for replacing previously load-bearing components when scale and data permit.
